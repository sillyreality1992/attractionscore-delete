Account & Data Deletion – AttractionScore

Users of the AttractionScore mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

Email us at: firedragon11091992@gmail.com
Subject: Delete My AttractionScore Account

Data that will be deleted

  • User account (email address and user ID)
  • Ratings and activity data
  • Uploaded image links, if any

Data that may be retained

  • Purchase and transaction records (as required by law)

Account and associated data are permanently deleted within 7–14 days after verification.
